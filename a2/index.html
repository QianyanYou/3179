Population in Australia

This website shows the population growth and changes in Australian cities and states in 2017.

Population in cites

The purpose of the census is to find out the changes in the number, composition and residence of the population, and to formulate detailed survey plans for different subjects. The birth rate, death rate and other population conditions are investigated by means of comprehensive survey and incomplete survey.

Population in States

In 2017, each Australian state added between 10,000 and 30,000 people, which is higher than the annual increase in the United States.

Growth Population

Between 2011 and 2017, Geelong saw the fastest growth, with a growth rate of more than 0.5 percent.
